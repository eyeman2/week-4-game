Game Name


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four consoles below. By clicking on a console you will add a specific amount of points ot your total score

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each console is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when a game starts, the game will change the values of each console
